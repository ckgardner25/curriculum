The electrical and computer engineers' most powerful tool in our aresenal is the oscilloscope. This machine is able to read voltage over time and perform calculations in some cases. To avoid long discussions of theory this guide will explain basic functions of the oscilloscope. There are two types of oscilloscopes digital and analog, in our labratory setting we use digital however, some engineers still perfer the accuracy of the analog machines.

## Basics
The purpose of the oscilloscope is to graph voltage over time, for accuracy of reading there are many controls that dominant the face of the machine.
